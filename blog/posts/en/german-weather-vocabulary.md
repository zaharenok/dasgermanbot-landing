# German Weather Vocabulary: Complete Guide to Wetter for Daily Conversations

You're learning German. You want to make small talk.

The weather is the perfect icebreaker. **Wie ist das Wetter?** How do you describe sunny, rainy, or stormy days?

German weather vocabulary (Wetter) is essential for daily conversations — from casual chats to planning activities.

**The solution:** Master German weather vocabulary with clear categories, proper articles, and practical phrases.

## Basic Weather Conditions

**Fundamental weather:**

### Sun and Clear Sky

1. **Die Sonne** (the sun)
   - **Use:** Light source
   - **Example:** "Die Sonne scheint." (The sun is shining.)

2. **Sonnig** (sunny)
   - **Use:** Beautiful weather
   - **Example:** "Heute ist es sonnig." (Today it is sunny.)

3. **Hell** (bright/clear)
   - **Use:** Clear sky
   - **Example:** "Der Himmel ist hell." (The sky is bright.)

4. **Klar** (clear)
   - **Use:** Clear weather
   - **Example:** "Es ist klar." (It is clear.)

### Clouds and Overcast

5. **Die Wolke** (the cloud)
   - **Use:** Sky formation
   - **Example:** "Eine Wolke am Himmel." (A cloud in the sky.)

6. **Wolkig** (cloudy)
   - **Use:** Overcast sky
   - **Example:** "Es ist wolkig." (It is cloudy.)

7. **Bewölkt** (overcast)
   - **Use:** Heavy clouds
   - **Example:** "Der Himmel ist bewölkt." (The sky is overcast.)

8. **Dunkel** (dark)
   - **Use:** Dark sky
   - **Example:** "Es wird dunkel." (It is getting dark.)

### Rain and Precipitation

9. **Der Regen** (the rain)
   - **Use:** Water falling
   - **Example:** "Es regnet." (It is raining.)

10. **Regnerisch** (rainy)
    - **Use:** Rainy weather
    - **Example:** "Heute ist es regnerisch." (Today it is rainy.)

11. **Der Regenschauer** (the rain shower)
    - **Use:** Short rain
    - **Example:** "Ein Regenschauer." (A rain shower.)

12. **Der Platzregen** (the downpour)
    - **Use:** Heavy rain
    - **Example:** "Es ist ein Platzregen." (It is a downpour.)

13. **Nass** (wet)
    - **Use:** Wet conditions
    - **Example:** "Es ist nass." (It is wet.)

### Snow and Cold

14. **Der Schnee** (the snow)
    - **Use:** Frozen precipitation
    - **Example:** "Es schneit." (It is snowing.)

15. **Schneeig** (snowy)
    - **Use:** Snowy weather
    - **Example:** "Es ist schneeig." (It is snowy.)

16. **Kalt** (cold)
    - **Use:** Low temperature
    - **Example:** "Es ist kalt." (It is cold.)

17. **Frost** (frost)
    - **Use:** Frozen ground
    - **Example:** "Es gibt Frost." (There is frost.)

18. **Eisig** (icy)
    - **Use:** Very cold
    - **Example:** "Es ist eisig." (It is icy.)

### Wind and Storms

19. **Der Wind** (the wind)
    - **Use:** Moving air
    - **Example:** "Der Wind weht." (The wind is blowing.)

20. **Windig** (windy)
    - **Use:** Windy weather
    - **Example:** "Es ist windig." (It is windy.)

21. **Der Sturm** (the storm)
    - **Use:** Severe weather
    - **Example:** "Es ist ein Sturm." (There is a storm.)

22. **Gewitter** (thunderstorm)
    - **Use:** Storm with lightning
    - **Example:** "Es gibt ein Gewitter." (There is a thunderstorm.)

23. **Der Blitz** (the lightning)
    - **Use:** Electric flash
    - **Example:** "Ein Blitzschlag." (A lightning strike.)

24. **Der Donner** (the thunder)
    - **Use:** Thunder sound
    - **Example:** "Es donnert." (It is thundering.)

### Fog and Mist

25. **Der Nebel** (the fog)
    - **Use:** Low visibility
    - **Example:** "Es ist neblig." (It is foggy.)

26. **Nebelig** (foggy)
    - **Use:** Foggy weather
    - **Example:** "Der Morgen ist neblig." (The morning is foggy.)

27. **Der Dunst** (the mist/haze)
    - **Use:** Light fog
    - **Example:** "Es ist diesig." (It is hazy.)

### Temperature Descriptions

**Hot and warm:**

28. **Heiß** (hot)
    - **Use:** Very warm
    - **Example:** "Es ist heiß." (It is hot.)

29. **Warm** (warm)
    - **Use:** Pleasant temperature
    - **Example:** "Es ist warm." (It is warm.)

30. **Mild** (mild)
    - **Use:** Moderate temperature
    - **Example:** "Ein milder Tag." (A mild day.)

**Cool and cold:**

31. **Kühl** (cool)
    - **Use:** Slightly cold
    - **Example:** "Es ist kühl." (It is cool.)

32. **Frostig** (freezing)
    - **Use:** Very cold
    - **Example:** "Es ist frostig." (It is freezing.)

33. **Eiskalt** (ice cold)
    - **Use:** Extremely cold
    - **Example:** "Es ist eiskalt." (It is ice cold.)

## Weather Phrases

**Essential expressions:**

34. **Wie ist das Wetter?** (How is the weather?)
    - **Use:** Asking about weather

35. **Es ist schön.** (It is beautiful.)
    - **Use:** Good weather

36. **Es ist schlecht.** (It is bad.)
    - **Use:** Bad weather

37. **Das Wetter ist gut.** (The weather is good.)
    - **Use:** Positive description

38. **Das Wetter ist schlecht.** (The weather is bad.)
    - **Use:** Negative description

39. **Wie wird das Wetter?** (What will the weather be?)
    - **Use:** Weather forecast

40. **Die Wettervorhersage sagt...** (The weather forecast says...)
    - **Use:** Forecast information

## Seasonal Weather

**Weather by season:**

41. **Im Frühling** (in spring)
    - **Use:** Spring season
    - **Example:** "Im Frühling blühen die Blumen." (In spring, flowers bloom.)

42. **Im Sommer** (in summer)
    - **Use:** Summer season
    - **Example:** "Im Sommer ist es heiß." (In summer, it is hot.)

43. **Im Herbst** (in autumn)
    - **Use:** Autumn season
    - **Example:** "Im Herbst fallen die Blätter." (In autumn, leaves fall.)

44. **Im Winter** (in winter)
    - **Use:** Winter season
    - **Example:** "Im Winter schneit es oft." (In winter, it often snows.)

## Weather Activities

**What to do:**

45. **Einen Spaziergang machen** (to go for a walk)
    - **Example:** "Bei schönem Wetter spazieren." (Walking in nice weather.)

46. **Schwimmen gehen** (to go swimming)
    - **Example:** "Im Sommer schwimmen." (Swimming in summer.)

47. **Skifahren** (to ski)
    - **Example:** "Im Winter Skifahren." (Skiing in winter.)

48. **Drinnen bleiben** (to stay inside)
    - **Example:** "Bei Regen drinnen bleiben." (Staying inside during rain.)

## Cultural Notes

### German Weather Conversations

- **Small talk:** Weather is a safe conversation starter
- **Complaint culture:** Germans love complaining about bad weather
- **Weather obsession:** Detailed weather discussions are common
- **Regional differences:** North vs. South weather patterns
- **Seasonal pride:** Each season has its advocates

### German Weather Sayings

- **"April, April, macht was er will"** (April does whatever it wants)
- **"Aller Anfang ist schwer"** (All beginnings are difficult — used for spring)
- **"Jungferngrün"** (Maiden's green — first spring growth)

## Common Mistakes to Avoid

### Mistake 1: Wrong Verb Form

❌ "Die Sonne scheinen" (incorrect)
✅ "Die Sonne scheint" (correct)

**Rule:** Weather verbs use third person singular.

### Mistake 2: Wrong Article

❌ "Der Regen" (incorrect)
✅ "Der Regen" (correct for masculine)

**Rule:** Learn the gender of weather nouns.

### Mistake 3: Confusing Similar Words

❌ Confusing "neblig" (foggy) with "diesig" (hazy)
✅ **Nebelig** = thick fog
✅ **Diesig** = light haze

## Weather Idioms

**German expressions:**

54. **Das ist nicht mein Wetter.** (That's not my kind of weather.)
    - **Meaning:** I don't like this situation

55. **Wetter wie im Fliegenband** (Weather like on an assembly line)
    - **Meaning:** Consistent, predictable weather

56. **Ein Gesicht wie sieben Tage Regenwetter** (A face like seven days of rain)
    - **Meaning:** Looking very unhappy

German weather vocabulary follows clear patterns:

1. **Basic conditions** — Sonnig, Wolkig, Regnerisch, Schneeig
2. **Precipitation** — Regen, Schnee, Hagel (hail), Nebel
3. **Temperature** — Heiß, Warm, Kühl, Kalt, Eisig
4. **Wind** — Windig, Stürmisch, Sturm
5. **Storms** — Gewitter, Blitz, Donner
6. **Seasons** — Frühling, Sommer, Herbst, Winter
7. **Phrases** — Wie ist das Wetter?, Es ist..., Das Wetter wird...
