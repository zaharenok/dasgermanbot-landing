# German Clothing Vocabulary: Complete Guide to Fashion and Shopping

You're learning German. You walk into a clothing store.

You need to ask for a shirt, pants, or shoes. But what are these words in German? How do you ask for a different size? Or a specific color?

German clothing vocabulary is essential for everyday life — from shopping to describing what you're wearing.

**The solution:** Master German clothing words with clear categories, proper articles, and practical shopping phrases.

## Basic Clothing Items

**Start with everyday essentials:**

### Tops and Shirts

1. **Das Hemd** (the shirt)
   - **Use:** Dress shirt, button-up
   - **Example:** "Ich brauche ein neues Hemd." (I need a new shirt.)

2. **Das T-Shirt** (the T-shirt)
   - **Use:** Casual shirt
   - **Example:** "Das T-shirt ist blau." (The T-shirt is blue.)

3. **Die Bluse** (the blouse)
   - **Use:** Women's dressy top
   - **Example:** "Sie trägt eine schöne Bluse." (She is wearing a beautiful blouse.)

4. **Das Pullover** (the sweater/pullover)
   - **Use:** Warm top
   - **Example:** "Der Pullover ist aus Wolle." (The sweater is made of wool.)

5. **Die Jacke** (the jacket)
   - **Use:** Outer layer
   - **Example:** "Ich brauche eine warme Jacke." (I need a warm jacket.)

6. **Der Mantel** (the coat)
   - **Use:** Heavy winter coat
   - **Example:** "Der Mantel ist zu lang." (The coat is too long.)

## Bottoms: Pants and Skirts

7. **Die Hose** (the pants/trousers)
   - **Plural:** Usually used in plural form
   - **Example:** "Die Hose passt mir nicht." (The pants don't fit me.)

8. **Die Jeans** (the jeans)
   - **Use:** Denim pants
   - **Example:** "Ich suche eine schwarze Jeans." (I'm looking for black jeans.)

9. **Der Rock** (the skirt)
   - **Use:** Women's skirt
   - **Example:** "Der Rock ist kurz." (The skirt is short.)

10. **Das Kleid** (the dress)
    - **Use:** One-piece dress
    - **Example:** "Das Kleid ist elegant." (The dress is elegant.)

## Underwear and Sleepwear

11. **Die Unterwäsche** (underwear)
    - **Use:** General term
    - **Example:** "Ich brauche neue Unterwäsche." (I need new underwear.)

12. **Der Slip** (panties/briefs)
    - **Use:** Underwear
    - **Example:** "Die Slips sind im Angebot." (The underwear is on sale.)

13. **Das Nachthemd** (nightgown)
    - **Use:** Sleepwear
    - **Example:** "Das Nachthemd ist bequem." (The nightgown is comfortable.)

14. **Der Pyjama** (pajamas)
    - **Use:** Sleepwear
    - **Example:** "Ich trage Pyjama zum Schlafen." (I wear pajamas for sleeping.)

## Footwear

## Shopping Phrases

**Essential phrases for clothing shopping:**

41. **Ich suche...** (I'm looking for...)
    - **Example:** "Ich suche eine Jacke." (I'm looking for a jacket.)

42. **Haben Sie...?** (Do you have...?)
    - **Example:** "Haben Sie das in Rot?" (Do you have this in red?)

43. **Kann ich es anprobieren?** (Can I try it on?)
    - **Use:** Fitting room request

44. **Wo ist die Umkleidekabine?** (Where is the fitting room?)
    - **Use:** Finding fitting room

45. **Das passt mir nicht.** (That doesn't fit me.)
    - **Use:** Poor fit

46. **Das ist zu klein/groß.** (That is too small/big.)
    - **Use:** Size issue

47. **Wie viel kostet das?** (How much does that cost?)
    - **Use:** Price inquiry

48. **Kann ich mit Karte bezahlen?** (Can I pay by card?)
    - **Use:** Payment method

## Cultural Notes

### German Shopping Culture

- **Return policy:** Generous return policies (usually 14-30 days)
- **Quality:** Germans value quality over quantity
- **Seasonal shopping:** Sales follow seasonal patterns (Sommerschlussverkauf, Winterschlussverkauf)
- **Cash vs card:** Cash is still common, but cards are widely accepted

### Dress Code

- **Casual:** Everyday wear is casual
- **Business:** Formal business attire is expected in offices
- **Restaurants:** Smart casual is typical for dining out

## Common Mistakes to Avoid

### Mistake 1: Wrong Article

❌ "Der Hose" (incorrect)
✅ "Die Hose" (correct)

**Rule:** "Hose" is feminine despite ending in -e.

### Mistake 2: Singular vs Plural

❌ "Ein Hose" (incorrect)
✅ "Eine Hose" (correct)

**Rule:** "Hose" looks singular but takes singular article. Can be used as plural "Hosen".

### Mistake 3: Wrong Preposition

❌ "An der Hose" (incorrect)
✅ "In der Hose" (correct)

**Rule:** Use "in" for wearing clothes, not "an".
