What data we collect

We use the collected data solely to provide and improve the bot's educational features. This includes personalizing lessons, tracking your progress, and responding to your questions about German language learning.

Your data is stored on secure servers. We retain your learning data for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide the service. You can request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us via Telegram.

The bot operates within Telegram and uses AI services to generate learning content. We do not sell, share, or transfer your personal data to third parties for marketing purposes.

This website may use cookies and Meta Pixel for analytics and advertising purposes. You can disable cookies in your browser settings at any time.

Your rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data. To exercise these rights, contact us via the bot or by email. We will respond within 30 days.

Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, reach out to us via @dasgermanbot on Telegram.
